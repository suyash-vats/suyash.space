And just like that, It was over

The Final Chapter

Suyash • Dec 28, 2024 • 93 views

This was the one thing I feared most. The one thing I ran from, the one thing I swore I’d never face again. Yet here it was, waiting for me in the shadows. That gnawing emptiness, the unfillable void—it wasn’t just back; it had been there all along, lurking beneath every fleeting moment of happiness.

I should have known. I should have known this was temporary, that the vibrant colors you painted my life with would fade. But I didn’t think it would happen so soon. I didn’t think it would hurt this much. The world we built—our little world of joy and fire—was crumbling. And I was the one tearing it apart, piece by piece.

Why now? Why, when I finally thought I had escaped this endless cycle? When I thought, for once, that I was _better_? I wanted to believe what we had was real, that it was different. But now I don’t know if I ever truly loved you, or if I was just chasing the high, the rush, the spark. And yet, it hurts—losing you still hurts in ways I can’t even explain.

You deserved so much more. Someone who could hold onto you, someone who wouldn’t turn away the moment the fire started to dim. But that wasn’t me. I was never that person. I didn’t know how to hold onto anything without breaking it.

You were my light in the dark, my fleeting glimpse of warmth in the cold. But even that wasn’t enough. And I hate myself for that. I hate myself for not being able to love you the way you deserved to be loved. You gave me everything, and all I could give back was my chaos, my emptiness, my hollow promises.

I wanted to tell you the truth. I wanted to let you go gently, to spare you the pain. But how do you break someone’s heart softly? How do you tear apart something so fragile without shattering it completely?

And now, as I sit here with nothing but memories, I can feel the weight of my own selfishness crushing me. You’re gone, and I’m the reason why. You’ll never know how much I wish I could have been different—how much I wish I could have stayed.

But the truth is, I’ve always been like this. Hollow, restless, desperate to feel something, anything, no matter who gets hurt in the process. I didn’t need fixing. I needed to escape the void inside me, even if it meant losing you.

Now all I have left is the ghost of what we were, haunting me in the quiet hours of the night. You were my brightest moment, and I let you slip away. And as much as I try to move on, a part of me will always stay behind, lost in the memory of you.

I’m sorry. I’m sorry I couldn’t be the person you needed. I’m sorry I let you love me when I couldn’t love you back the way you deserved. And I’m sorry that, even now, all I can think about is how empty I still feel without you.

You were my everything. And I lost you.
